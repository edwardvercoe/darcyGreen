Experience "The visceral thrill of being throttled by razor-sharp guitars, slapped around the head by thundering drums and screamed at by a man gargling glass." – Rolling Stone Magazine

The film clip for I Am Horror by Captives follows two guests and a receptionist and their troubling overnight stay at an eerie hotel.

Working with the Musical Theatre students from NIDA, this cabaret showcased and reimagined 1920’s Berlin and the Weimar era.

Performed at the Erskineville Hotel, these performances recreated famous songs and stories from stars like Shakira, Shania Twain, and Julie Andrews.
