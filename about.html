Darcy is a theatre and film director from Sydney.

In 2015 Darcy completed a Bachelor of Design in Visual Communications at the University of Technology Sydney. Darcy has also completed a diploma of screen performance at WAAPA in 2016. Before undertaking his Masters of Fine Arts (Directing) at NIDA, Darcy worked in advertising and branding; working across print, digital and video content.

As a director, Darcy is interested in Australian stories and particularly new Australian work. He is also interested in combining his passions for design and film to enhance a live theatrical performance.
